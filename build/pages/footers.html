Footers

Footer refers to the section located at the bottom of a webpage or application screen. It typically contains information, navigation links, or additional functionality that is consistent across multiple pages or screens within the interface. Footers serve as a way to anchor and organize content, provide important information, and enhance user navigation and engagement.
