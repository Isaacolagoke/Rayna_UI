Badges

Badges help highlight important information, such as notifications or new and unread messages. They’re primarily used for communicating secondary or additional information to text.
Use the button components as desired. I have provided a variety of styles and button sizes as designed by Rayna. You can use Tailwind styling for the padding or copy the Rayna CSS styling.
